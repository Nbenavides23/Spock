Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931 - 2015

Leonard Nimoy was actor, author,director,singer and photographer. His most we known role was that of Spock in the Star Trek franchise. The character is a half-vulcan half-human star fleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the UUS Enterprise, they explore the far reaches of space. Leonard portrayed Spock from 1964, in Start Trek: The Original Series to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into Darkness.
